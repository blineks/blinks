All features

  • Powerful 3-in-1 cleaning

    The Dyson Digital Slim™ vacuum transforms easily from a stick vacuum to a handheld to perform a range of tasks that could otherwise need three separate machines.

  • 40% smaller and lighter Slim Fluffy™ cleaner head3

    Made from high-tensile ABS for strength and lightness, the Slim Fluffy™ cleaner head uses soft woven nylon to capture large debris, while black anti-static carbon fibre filaments sweep fine particles from hard floors.

  • Edge-to-edge cleaning

    The motor is integrated into the brush bar, allowing uninterrupted edge-to-edge cleaning.

  • Intelligently reports in real time

    LCD screen shows current performance, including selected power mode, remaining run time, filter maintenance reminders and blockage reports.

  • Easy to maintain

    The filter and Fluffy™ brush bar can be washed, and the bin and tools wiped clean, to remove dust build-up and help optimise your Dyson vacuum’s performance.

  • Smooth bin emptying

    The straight, slim bin has a hygienic ejection mechanism with a rotary catch, to drive out dust in one smooth action.

  • Wall dock

    The Dyson Digital Slim™ vacuum drops into the wall-mounted charging dock, ready for your next clean.

  • Additional click-in battery and charger available to buy seperately

    Remove and replace at the press of a button to double your run time.

  • Light pipe crevice tool

    Crevice tool with LED lights to show you where you’re cleaning, even in dark spaces.

  • Dyson Hyperdymium™ motor

    Small, light and powerful, this Dyson Hyperdymium™ motor spins at up to 120,000rpm to generate 100 Air Watts of powerful suction.

  • Highly-efficient scrolled cyclones

    Generating over 100,000g, the lightweight cyclone pack has 11 scrolled cyclones, engineered to remove fine dust and dirt from the airflow.

  • Advanced, whole machine filtration

    Fully-sealed, five-layer filtration system captures dust and allergens, and keeps them trapped in the bin. So it emits cleaner air4.
